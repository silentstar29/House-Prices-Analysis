Pearson Correlation Matrix

Running a Pearson correlation matrix on the data to find the parameters that have the largest impact on Sale Price will help prioritize the data and assist with the most meaninguful visualizations.

Conversion to log10

Converting the data to log10 was found to decrease sigma, which helps improve the accuracy of the model. Once the model has been completed, the results should be transformed again.
